Italy — The Biggest Shock
4-time World Cup winners — missing their 3rd consecutive tournament

Italy missed the 2018, 2022 and now 2026 World Cups — the first World Cup-winning nation ever to miss three tournaments in a row. What makes it worse: the 2026 edition has 48 teams, making qualification easier than ever.

3
Consecutive World Cups missed
4
World Cup titles (all-time)

Italy finished 2nd in qualifying behind Norway and lost their UEFA playoff final to Bosnia and Herzegovina on penalties. The Italian football chief subsequently resigned.

Can India Qualify for 2030?

The 2030 World Cup will again be 48 teams with 8 AFC slots. The door stays equally wide open — India just needs to be ready to walk through it.

What India needs to change

📈
Reach top 100 in FIFA rankings
Better seeding avoids Japan, South Korea, Iran, Saudi Arabia in the group stage — the difference between possible and near-impossible.
🏟️
Expand ISL match volume
India plays ~15 league matches per season vs 35+ in qualifying nations. More competitive minutes means better players.
⭐
Post-Chhetri generation must step up
Sunil Chhetri retired in 2024. The 2017 U-17 World Cup generation needs to mature into senior stars by 2028–30.
🎯
Favourable group draw
With 8 AFC spots from ~46 nations, India needs to avoid the powerhouses in round 2 qualifying groups.
