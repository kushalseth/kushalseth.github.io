B2B SaaS Blockchain Compliance

Agent Provenance Ledger

Tamper-proof audit trails for enterprise AI agents

Enterprises deploy AI agents that approve loans, execute trades, send legal notices — but when something goes wrong, they have no way to prove what the agent did, why, or who is responsible. Regulators don't trust mutable server logs. Courts want signed evidence.

B2B infrastructure that makes AI agent actions legally provable to parties outside your organization. Every tool call, model invocation, and decision output is cryptographically signed with the agent's DID and anchored to an immutable chain. Only hashes go on-chain — raw data stays encrypted in your storage.

SDK drops into any Python/JS agent framework in under an hour
Full decision reconstruction — every input, intermediate step, and model version
Pre-formatted for EU AI Act Article 12, SOC2, and MiFID II
Exportable as signed legal evidence packages

Blockchain · Creator Tools
Planned
B2C Browser Ext Auth

TrueVoice

Prove your writing is human. Permanently.

AI can now produce content indistinguishable from human work. Creators can't prove their work is theirs. A freelance copywriter losing contracts. A student flagged 75% AI by Turnitin — with no appeal. A journalist who can't verify a source document.

Not an AI detector. A proof system. As you write, TrueVoice captures cryptographic fingerprints at intervals — plus keystroke cadence and revision patterns, the behavioral signature of a human writing. Anchored on-chain. The certificate is yours permanently — no company can revoke it, not even us.

Behavioral biometrics — impossible to fake retroactively (AI generates 1,000 words in 3 seconds; humans take 45 minutes with pauses and restructuring)
One-click dispute package for university appeals, DMCA claims, and platform challenges
Creator-owned certificate — share a public verification link with editors or clients

AI · Dev Tooling
Planned
Dev Tool AI Memory SaaS

ContextVault

Persistent, structured AI context across every session

Every time you start a new chat, you're the human API — manually re-dumping everything you know about your project into a blank conversation. AI has amnesia and you pay the tax. The older the project, the worse it gets.

Context should live somewhere persistent, structured, and AI-readable — not inside a human's memory. ContextVault stores your tech stack decisions, architecture rationale, what you already tried, and how projects relate to each other. When you open a new chat about any project, the AI already knows what it needs to know — without you having to explain anything.

Captures decisions across time, breadth (multiple projects), and depth (layered reasoning)
Exports structured context files that load into Claude, ChatGPT, or any AI tool instantly
Never re-explains your stack, your constraints, or why you ruled something out

Univally

AI-driven university matching & verifiable credential platform

Students navigate fragmented admissions processes with no unified profile. Universities manage applications across disconnected systems. Credentials are slow, paper-based, and untrusted. Nobody has a single source of truth — and regulators are demanding one.

A cloud-native platform that digitises the entire student journey — from discovery to admission, verification, and credential lifecycle. Students get a unified digital wallet (academic records, achievements, preferences). Universities manage applications and offers through standardised workflows. Elina AI guides students. Microservices architecture — Next.js, NestJS, CosmosDB, Azure Service Bus, Redis

Launchpad

One dashboard to replace five tools for indie makers

Launching a landing page today means Plausible for analytics, Formspark for forms, UptimeRobot for monitoring, Bitly for links, and a GDPR consent tool — five accounts, five bills, none talking to each other. Makers pay $27–50/mo for a worse solution than they'd get from one coherent product.

Launchpad is the Notion of launch infrastructure — analytics, form handling, uptime monitoring, link management, and cookie consent in one product. Replaces Plausible + Formspark + UptimeRobot + Bitly + Cookiebot in one dashboard
